- sales deal blocked by a customer security questionnaire;

One sentence:
> Before you buy a full GRC platform, turn one customer questionnaire into a reusable answer map for $99.

## Redaction checklist to send after interest

Before sending anything, remove:
- customer names and contractual terms;
- secrets, API keys, credentials, network diagrams, IPs;
- screenshots with sensitive internal data;
- employee/customer personal data;
- unreleased product/security details that should not leave the company.

Acceptable first pass:
- column headings;
- question text;
- blank answer cells;
- generic policy/control descriptions the company is comfortable sharing;
- current draft answers they already send to prospects.

## Prospect qualification questions

1. Is a current or near-term sales deal blocked by this questionnaire?
2. How many questions / rows / portal sections?
3. Do you already have an answer library?
4. Is this a one-off or have you seen repeated customer questions?
5. Are you looking for a first-draft map, or for compliance/security advice? If the latter, disqualify.
6. Can you safely redact the questionnaire before sharing?
